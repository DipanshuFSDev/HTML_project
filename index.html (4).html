MY top movies of all time



1.Tanhaji

tells the story of Tanhaji Malusare, a loyal military leader under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who is tasked with recapturing the strategically important Kondhana fort (now known as Sinhagad) from the Mughal Empire, currently guarded by the fierce Rajput commander Udaybhan Rathod, leading to a fierce battle where Tanaji sacrifices his life to achieve victory for the Marathas.


2.Marco

The plot follows Marco, a member of a crime mafia, who seeks revenge on the brutal killing of his blind stepbrother Victor.


3.Salaar

tells a thrilling story about power struggles and revenge.
